Two popular multiplayer games are Grand Theft Auto V and Counter-Strike 2.

Grand Theft Auto V, often called GTA 5, is an open-world action game from Rockstar Games. It includes a story campaign and GTA Online, where players drive vehicles, complete missions, buy properties, and explore Los Santos with other players.

Counter-Strike 2, often called CS2, is a tactical first-person shooter from Valve. It focuses on team-based matches where terrorists and counter-terrorists compete around objectives such as bomb planting, bomb defusal, and site control.
